My Little Nephew

By Jesus Negrete

Eliseo

I present to you my little nephew, Eliseo. He was born on June 14, 2020. He is a very funny baby and a fast learner. He likes to go to my room and makes a mess. He is intelligent because whenever he sees me in the kitchen he runs to my room knowing that I left my door open, so he can go in. He loves attention and he loves us and we love him back.

Copycater

He's also a imitator, for example, he sees where we throw away the trash, so when we give him a box to throw away he knows where exaclty to go. Whenever he sees someone waving at him, he waves back. He would start start to dance if he see someone dancing, he would scream somebody else screams, and he would clap his hands if he see someone clapping their hands.
